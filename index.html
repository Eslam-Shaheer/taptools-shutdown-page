Thank You for Being Part of Our Journey

TapTools has paused operations.

Over the years, TapTools became an important part of the Cardano ecosystem, serving over a million users and supporting projects across the community.

While the platform is no longer active, we continue to believe TapTools still holds significant value for the Cardano ecosystem and are exploring opportunities for its next chapter.

This may be the end of one chapter, but we hope it is not the end of the TapTools story.

Thank you for your trust, your support, and for helping us build something that mattered.
